Michael "MadSkills84" Pistone title: ! madskills-in-milford-ma-5-5-01 tags: - Tournament_Report date: 2001-05-08 --- Well it all happened a month ago when I heard the news I’ve been waiting to hear........."Video Connection closing, Milford will take over the tournaments" SWEET! For all of you who don’t know, Video Connection was a tiny movie rental place with a few tables so it doubled up as a card store. Everyone used to go to these crappy tournaments too, so we’d cram 25 smelly star wars player at like tables that fit 10. They decided to add more tables to a small area, which did not solve this cramming problem, and conversely made it more cramped. Then genius idea they had, we got extra tables (cuz people stopped going to the Star Wars tournaments cuz they sucked) so we’ll have magic tournaments the same day. Oh god no, if Star Wars players reeked Magic players were worse. This and the fact the prize support was Cloud City packs was enough for me to not waste my time with these tournaments much. Well neways, end of that rant, my friend Jared Greenwald decided to pick up the monthly trend at a store called TJ’s in Milford which is a sweet place for tournaments, plenty of room, fresh air. Ahhhh. Seriously all you old Video Connection players should go play there, the tournaments are sweet.

Well as far as my decks went, I was playing my version of Steven Lewis’ Palace Raiders and I decided I’d trade some promo foils get up to 5 Emperors and try Dark Surgeon out with some SAC in the spirit of what Kyle Craft used at the DPC.

So i’m talking to my friend Hayes Hunter, who happens to be number 3 in the world (cheap pop), and he tells me that he is gonna go to the tournament just to sell some cards to make money for Tatooine but he’s not gonna play. Hayes has talked about cutting down the amount of SWCCG tournaments he plays in and increasing the Legend Of The Five Rings tournaments, so I thought nothing of it and told him my decks and had him help me make a few changes to them so they could be more effective. Cool

Well the day before the tournament the kid I was swapping rides with, Rich Hinds, ended up telling me that he wasn’t gonna be able to go. Oh how @#$%ing wonderful. Now I was out of a ride and I had to find a way to the tournament. Well it looked like I was losing hope so I just asked Rich won more time online if he could hook me up with a ride. After telling him i’d pay for his gas and build his decks apparently his dad could give us a ride and he didn’t need decks anymore. Strange how that all spun around in a circle to what we originally agreed on. :-/

Well anyways I was psyched that I got to go to the tournament, we got a little lost from mapquest, but we got there ok. We had 17 participants then Hayes decided he’d play. Well that was cool no byes and a really good player too.

Pretournament Rating: 1581 (with 3-1 not in)

Game 1:
WYS Palace Raiders vs. Paul Maholick’s (1645) Hunt Down 7’s

Well Paul is a really cool guy who I have played in a few games before and the results are pretty much split, with him winning a few more than me. I knew he was playing the 7’s version before the game even started because he was going around asking to trade for Visages etc. Well before we started he tells me he decided he’d play two fun decks just for the hell of it which is pretty cool of him to do that and not be as competitive about the game as everyone else. Well his first turn he pulls Carida and the Executor: Docking Bay. He drew his force pile, most likely realizing he had a Vader in there when he saw his deck. We each lose one to Visage. My turn I activate, pull spaceport docking bay, pull falcon drop it move it up to system, draw the rest and trans term visage. He goes activates, pulls cloud city docking bay, drops vader, flips, drops visage and drops prince xizor with vader. I lost one to visage, i think it was a control/tunnel vision off the top. I activated, drained for one at the system, he lost a circle from hand. I looked at the epp luke in my hand, realized i had a lightsaber, he didn’t and my destinies are pretty good. I dropped epp luke to the cantina hoping he would blind duel. He did. I drew a 4 and a 3, him a 3 and a 2. I win. Cool, vader went out of play and he lost 9. I then deployed a palace raider and a dash rendar to the site, flipping my objective. I battle Xizor, hit him, make him forfeit zero. Draw 2 battle destiny, he ended up losing like another 10 to the battle. So now he has like about 20 cards or so in his hand. We each lost one to visage. He activated his turn and just drew. We each lost one to visage. I activate, deploy a palace raider on a patrol craft. I play Tatooine Celebration. Basically that was game, I drained him repeatedly and retrieved and he eventually drew up.
FW +29

Highs: Winning a blind duel
Lows: Having the blind duel cost my friend so much.
