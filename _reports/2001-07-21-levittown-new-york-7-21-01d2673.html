Pre Tournament Stuff

Well the night before (Friday the 20th) my cousins slept over my house for Saturday&#8217;s tournament. This is their second tournament and since they don&#8217;t have to many good cards I make their decks. We play test until around 11:30, and I&#8217;m a little bit nervous with my dark deck, but since I figure I wont be able to make much better I stick with it. So we get to Tri-County about 10-15 min. early so we just sit around. My cousins buy some packs but didn&#8217;t pull anything special, each got a padme, but that&#8217;s about it. As time goes on more people show up and there is a total of 17 people, which means Brad Vogel the TD couldn&#8217;t play, stupid TD rules. Also going into this tournament my rating is a low 1438

My Decks:

LS: QMC: nothing special about it, just pretty solid.
DS: TTO: As I said before I was nervous with this but I had faith in it.

Game 1: My LS against Eric Vanderbeck&#8217;s (1497) Dark Deal
Eric is my cousin, so I knew I would have a 1st game win (not b/c of his playing skills but b/c this is my deck, and I know my QMC can beat it). Nothing big happened this game, he took over the sector and system early and I had the sites, but then I came out with ten numb in ship and wedge in ship with a x-wing laser cannon and intruder missile (can&#8217;t forget that lovely card Power Pivot) to knock him off Bespin. So I got &#8220;Cloud City Celebration&#8221; going retrieving 5 force a turn, and with my Paths and power on Cloud City he couldn&#8217;t set up dark deal or knock me offthe sites. So I won by 27
2(+27)

Lows:

Game 2: My DS against Matt Ehrhart&#8217;s (1947) RST racing
As soon as I saw him take the RST objective out of his deck, I knew I was in big trouble. Whether it was racing or just a plain RST I knew I had to build that ball fast. First turn I get Moff. Jerr. to the bunker with &#8220;Endor Shield&#8221; out, and I begin to search for my stuff. On his turn, he deploys LSJK to the back door and moves him to the bunker. My turn I know Jerr. is pretty much screwed so I pull the second sector then move him to the landing platform. His turn down comes Qui-Gon, with saber battles I lose Jerr. and a few overflow. So now I&#8217;m wondering how the hell am I supposed to get this thing up, I haven&#8217;t pulled Commander Merrejk yet to get systems out, and all I have out is Endor and D*2. So all I have is a drain of 2. He attempts to blow the bunker like 4 times before he finally blows it, so I have to lose 8 force and he retrieves 8 force. After that I finally get Merrejk to the podrace arena and he pulls out Sullust. He wins the podrace, so I lose 6 force, and he retrieves 6 force, which gets him back his whole lost pile (unbeknownst to me at the time when I lost my 6 cards off the top of my reserve the D*2 reactor core was in the group). So I know I lost this one, and all I&#8217;m going for is a lower loss differential. He deploys EPP Obi to the podrace arena and battles Merrejk, so I now lose even more force. I have a pilot in hand so I attempt to find the ship (with Combat Response), but it isn&#8217;t in there, while I was looking I noticed the Reactor Core wasn&#8217;t in there, so I&#8217;m thinking its in my force pile, I draw all the cards in my force pile, and am shocked that I don&#8217;t have it. After that he just drained me out. After the game I look in my lost pile and notice It&#8217;s in the middle of the pile, stupid me. So I lose by 39!!!!!!!

Game 3: My LS against Peter Purpura&#8217;s (1489) RalOps
So this game my third effect instead of Wise Advice combo was Ounee ta, just for the extra activation. The beginning was uneventful, pretty much he flipped his objective and I flipped mine. He deploys Vader, Tarkin, and 4Lom with gun to CC, I spend a lot of the game running from them, using Paths, Narrow Escapes, and regular movement, but always making sure I occupied at least 3 sites, for some good retrieval with celebration. In between this time I try to off the edge but I fail twice. I&#8217;ve never missed a off the edge before, usually I track a 7 or 6 or other decent destinys but this time I draw two ones. I blame it on me constantly having to use Keeping The Empire Out Forever and reshuffling my deck, but oh well. So I keep running around avoiding contact, so the tide of the game wouldn&#8217;t turn on me. Then when I finally manage to get all the remaining low destinys out of my deck, he drains me then draws up so I cant retreive any more. So I win by 12
4(0)
