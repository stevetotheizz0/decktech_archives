Now on to the games


Game 1 vs. Dann Albright

my Throne Room Main vs. his Hunt Down

I start the game with a decent hand, but nothing great. I activate 4 force, search for Threepio with His parts showing and get him to the Throne Room and draw the rest.
During his turn I start the race. While he starts getting out sites. For a while after that, he is just getting sites out and deploying some characters to the CC: Docking Bay and Podrace Arena. I get my sites out and start saving up for my Beat Down (as I have a few EPP’s in my hand). I later win the race, but thanks to CHYBC I retrieve nothing. He however looses 6 force. We are both loosing 1 a turn to Visage and I am loosing a couple to his drains. I decide the time is right to strike, so I deploy to the Theed Palace Generator with EPP HAN, EPP OBI, EPP Luke and Twas Khaa. Then the next turn I Narun Leids them to the Arena, and battle his characters. (Guri, Dr. E & Ponda Baba and 1-2 others)

I clear out the site, but Obi dies. His turn he gets Maul with Saber to the Generator. After that it’s me loosing 3 a turn to drains, and him loosing only 1 to drains, and we are both loosing to visage. I wind up loosing by 1.
