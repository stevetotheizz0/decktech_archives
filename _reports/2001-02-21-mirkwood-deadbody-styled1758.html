Game 1 my LLS vs. Herb and his HDADTJ.

Okay I have said a few not so nice things about Herb in the past but over the last few weeks he has gotten much less objectionable to play. Not fun mind you, I just hate him less now than I ever did. This game was quick and painful. I Twi’leked for Honor, and found out it wasn’t in my deck. Herb flipped on the 2nd turn with Vader Tarkin Mara at the Endor landing site, and the only other site deployed was the Hoth DB. We had a few battles, but Visage killed me quick. FL by 33.

highs. It was quick
lows: where to begin???? the entire game. This one loss almost made me not post this TR

Game 2 My DS. vs. Louie and his I don’t know.

Okay I think he was playing New age Throne room with DB’s and mains and SA, but he never really did anything. I play this BHBM without SA since it allows for more characters, and he burned at least 12 cards SAing my stuff which just went to my used pile. I got early Janus and Palpy at the Endor DB, the battles were very few. Vader and Tarkin Took on Obi and Mirax once. (maybe twice.) and Mara and Xixor paid a visit to the Home 1 db to see their pal wedge. Mara got in the way of the door but xixor (thanks to a tracked 5) taught Wedge the power of the black sun. at this point I had 3 cards stacked, but he had given me JKLuke so I just took him over and started dueling to try and make him lose 3. Late game he sent Twass Kah to take on Ephant Mon and Janus. Lost the battle, and the game since that gave me the last card I needed to stack, and I turned Luke.

FW +22, now 2(-11)
Highs: Turning Luke this deck is solid
Lows: Um.. Mara got in the way of as door. He sensed my Circle, so I played No escape to get it back, then he sensed it again. cost him 6 force, and as I write this I couldn’t do that, since it should have gone to my used pile.

Okay opening hand I got boussh. so I never took damage from court. I just spread out with my speeders and X-wings and out drained him. NO BATTLES. a late all wings for 8 helped up differential FW by 15 now 4(+4)

Highs: Rich is a fun opponent and this was a close game
lows: this is the first game I’ve had in a long time with no battles.
