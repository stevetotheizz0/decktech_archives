game 1, DS vs Brian Herold’s RST podracing (1790s)

i spend the first couple turns setting up on Tatooine, get out mosep and gailid at the AC so he’s losing 6 off the top every turn. he wins the podrace, and he sets up his guys in the bunker ready to blow it up. i come down with iggy, snoova with ax, Fett and some other guy to fight him. he’s cancelling one of my destinies with gen han and chewie and i’m cancelling his destiny with myo, but i’m drawing more battle destinies than he is and i’ve got guys with guns so eventually i win the site, but he uses the explosive charges to clear the site. he then comes down to AC with quigon, puts an explosive charge there, battles and then clears that site too. i lose gailid and mosep but he’s got so few cards in hand that i just spread out, i’ve got guys at all 4 tatooine sites and a guy at back door and a guy at rebel landing site, and i drain him out before he draws for more guys. win by 21.

game 3, DS vs Kasper’s EBO kewtol.

this game is short and sweet. i don’t have the cards for a turn 2 flip, and not finding the lower corridor prevents me from a turn 3 flip, so i am forced to set up on turn 4, which is ridiculously late for this deck. so i’m set up with guys at 3 sites and mosep and gailid. his turn he tunnle visions twice, gets out epp han and leia at the north ridge, and elis them over to the AC, battle. i don’t know what he was thinking but he miss both times with the blasters and i lose only ephant mon. he has to lose one guy but not both so he loses han and moves leia over to JP. well on my turn 5 guys come out and he loses 20 cards off the top, he spends the rest of the game drawing up, win by 28.

he starts court, mob points, and some other random effects. so i have to pull out DDTA as my third effect. his first turn he gets tatooine out and the stinger goes there, and he pulls the executor with mob points. i ponder what he is doing ’cos he must noticed i started with squassin and strike planning right? :-) i’m guessing he’s got a ghhhk in hand and hopes to lure me out and beat on the falcon with zuckuss next turn. well i do get the falcon and drop them at tatooine, battle, he loses stinger and a couple more, and i move the falcon to rendezvou point. so we both forget court the first turn. his turn tempest one and igar comes out to the sarlaac pit. my turn nein nub goes to falcon, i get Luke w/ saber to his AC, and the falcon moves to endor. his turn jabba comes out of his reserve and ephant mon from his hand to the AC, he battles, swing at ephant and hit, since i cant target jabba. so ephant is the only one that dies for him and luke dies. my turn epp obi and luke come out, jabba ghhks and obi dies. he gets out the executor at tatooine and moves piett up there. my turn i get OTE out and puts down another obi and corran horn at the AC to back luke up. eventually he gets out zuckuss and dengar at tatooine, and moves them over to endor, where i have falcon and pulsar skate. i just drew wedge’s ship for destiny the last turn, so i activate to it, draw it with mirax, finish activating, put wedge in his ship, and a xwing cannon on that. battle and shoot down dengar, power pivot executor. he loses dengar and everyone he had on the executor, while i lose no one. leg. starfighter goes on the falcon. his turn another couple pilots go to executor, he battles, draws a 6 so i have to lose lando to attrition while he loses the pilots and moves the executor back to tatooine. i chase him and the lat. damage and ote damage eats into him, i also had HB flipped so he doesn’t get any drains. i win by 19 or so.
