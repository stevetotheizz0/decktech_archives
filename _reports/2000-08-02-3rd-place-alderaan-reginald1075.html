After comicon, i wasnt sure if i was going to be going to reginals, but i was playtesting a lot anyways. I hade a few LS decks made but none of them did any good, so i just played a There is good in him scout deck.

The Decks:

LS: There is good in him, starts strike planning, staging areas, and insurrection. Pull a 1/0 docking bay and stick madine there and get lots of force activation, and pull out my scouts, i had 3 clouds for ralltiir and a total of 8 ships.

DS: Ralltiir ops, starting mobile points, IAO, and oppressive. Ill pulle weekimui (sp) and 1/0 docking bays and stick a scrub there to get the activation. I had the emp and his goons.


Game 1, LS vs Ken's Raithal Ops.
He starts the objective and bad feeling have i. First turn i pull the Home 1 docking bay and drop madine and panno for back up. His turn he just draws and drops like 6 sites. My second turn i activate 14 force and deploy Home 1 to raithal (thought he might be playing numbers or something).
He drops op and vader at one site and an op and a guy at another. I pull my second general, solo, and pull COK with madine and send them at vader and op, and send obi at the other pair, and clear both sites. He drops some lone ops and 3 sites, not knowing he cant drain. i have a drain of 8 going and i soon drain him out.

2(+33)

Highs: winning and a very fun game, for me atleast.
Lows: none for me.
