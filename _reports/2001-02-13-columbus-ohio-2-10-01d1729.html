Great, first game and Im going against Jamie. Im starting No Bargain, TiNT/Op. Enforcement, and Power of The Hutt. He starts Insurrection, Staging Areas, and Strike Planning. First turn I get Mighty Jabba and Ephant to Audience Chamber. He pulls his 2 Generals, Madine and Solo, and I drop JP from hand, go for Lower Passages and I got all
my sites out first turn :-) He activates, deploys Tatooine DBay, loses to Court and draws for a while. I drop Scum and Boelo to AC and draw some. He finally does something and
deploys Madine to Carkoon, pulls Geezum and moves to Hut. Mara and Dr. E follow, battle, retrieve 2. Swing at Madine....1...2.....wouldnt have made a difference. He
jumped the gun and played Swing and a Miss, but took it back cause it wasnt enough anyway....he loses Madine and Dr E bites is, who gets nail by an Out of Commission. Oh
well. So he continues to draw and stuff while losing to a Obi Hut drain of 4 and 1 at the AC. I drop Snoova and Ax to Dbay where Geezum is, he gets a Barrier, move Mara over.
He drops Ben and Gen Solo to Hut and drops a Soro Suub and moves Geezum away. Im still going around draining, and he is now too. He deploys Harc Seff and is like cancelling all of my drains, and apparently Hutt Influence doesnt prevent that. :-( After a few battles he gets me and drops Order To Engage and moves to meet up with Jodo and Mosep...a very non interactive game with him tracking and retrieving every turn using Figrin.

Game 2 - Light vs. Joe Shnaudts [1500] Bring Him Before Me

most of his cards to, and I remind him to force drain, cause I mean, theres no point in me just beating this kid. I would rather help him out than feel bad about it afterwards. He put up a decent fight. We both get set up and drops Emperor and Sim Aloo to D*2 Dbay and draws. I get Luke, Rebel Scout (Yea, I play with one, just cause of BHBM) and his saber to Endor: Dbay and put the pressure on him to be captured. He sets up some Royal Guards and Pikes and moves them over with Sim Aloo to my Cloud City: Dbay. I draw, drop Ben and his saber to Tatooine: Dbay and Chewbacca Protector to Endor:Dbay. He deploys Lord Vader, Darths Saber (yea, the crappy one), captures Luke, deploys Snoova, cant find the Ax, he battles, hits Chewbacca, but doesnt make him forfeit 0, so he covers everything. Then I relocate them both to Throne Room. My turn I drop DOS, EPP
Han, and Orrimarko....Ya, I know I shouldnt but its like the 8th turn and we have 15 minutes left. I start tracking and playing On the Edge to boost my differential. I beat
Snoova for alot and my drains do him in and so does his flipped objective.

2 (+15)

Game 3 - My Light vs. Lee Fisks [1503] Agents of the Black Sun

Man, this was an Old Fashioned Star Wars game here folks and Ill tell you why at the end of the game detail. He starts by deploy Coruscant Dbay and Emperor there. I deploy
Tato: Dbay and Endor: Dbay from hand. He activates and draws for a while deploy some locations. I drop Lando with Ax and Tawss Khaa to Tatooine to put some pressure on him. He deploys like 2 Makurths and Jodo to Tat and moves Xizor there and flips. My turn I put down some cards to start tracking with and deploy Jedi Luke with his Saber and flip him back, move the Tatooine crew to Endor and swallow his drain of 1. He then drops like 2 Vigos, Bane Malar, and Snoova to Endor, doesnt battle, moves his people over. I drop Daughter and Anis saber, Ben and saber to Tat, EPP Han to Endor with Chewie Protector, move Ben over and let him go. He battles, uses 1 to Scream with Makurth, tries to Hidden Weapons Luke, draws a 1. I slice up Xizor, Snoova, and Bane, play Gift of the Mentor....my destinies were the ones I tracked 6 6 5 5...I end up with 60 to his 44, so he has to almost clear his site, except for Jodo and a Makurth I think. So I track the 6s back around play On the Edge twice, retrieve my lost pile and let him go. He knows the game is over either way so he battles my crew and ends the game. THIS was old fashioned Star Wars CCG. You get all your people at one location, Ill get all my people at one location and we battle till one of us dies :-)


Full Win By 40
