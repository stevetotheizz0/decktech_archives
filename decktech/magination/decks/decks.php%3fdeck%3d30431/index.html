      D'reshi Illusions 1.3 - Way better this time!
      Rating
      (3.63) | Read Reviews
      Author Ernie "ErnieIII" Richardson (Subscribe to this member)
      Description
      Its back and this time more effective, This new version has played 7 games and not lost yet! Or come close!
      Submitted September 30, 2003
      Cards
      MAGI

      Harresh: (Xala, Bottled City, Unmake)

      Ythra: (Sunburn)

      D'jarvish: (Sikra)

      CREATURES (18)

      3 Olum Digger
      3 Uban
      3 Bulabantu
      3 Darbok
      2 Xala
      2 Sikra
      1 Ormagon
      1 Sandstorm orshaa

      SPELLS (16)

      3 Unmake
      3 Crushing Sands
      3 Impact
      3 Sunburn
      3 Forgotten dreams
      1 Heat of day

      RELICS (6)

      3 Warriors boots
      2 Climbing Staff
      1 Bottled City

      "Dreshi Illusions 1.3"

      TOTAL: 40 non-magi Cards 3 Magi
      By: ErnieIII
      Strategy
      NOTE** - I just submitted a deck called Dreshi Illusions but I messed up on the strategy and the name! I forgot the 1.3....So please totally IGNORE that deck and check this version out.


      Right now this deck has been pretty successful. I believe that I am playing the deck correctly and to ensure that I am I will write up my strategy in as much detail as I feel is needed for you to understand my thinking. What I am looking for right now is just to make this deck play better. Whatever reason you think that will make this deck better I would like to know. But Please read my Strategy so you will fully understand this deck and how to make it better. Thanks for your time!

      GENERAL STRATEGY FOR MAGI

      Harresh - Probably one of the best starting magi in the game. She can really come out swinging hard, and from what I know it takes a few turns to get any control over the board. But her amazing starting hand proves me wrong. I used to like going second so I had more energy to spend but Ive found going first is better. I mean your guaranteed an 8 energy creature and at least one other creature from bottled city first turn. But if you do go first do not take the unmake so you have better chances at drawing more creatures which is good. Time shuffle every turn you can and don’t hold back from taking out one of their magi. She is meant to take out a magi. Ive tooken out two with her because I play her so aggressively that my opponent has no time to setup. Do not worry about her being defeated if you have only 7- 10 cards in your hand. Seeing as your next magi can draw also.

      Cards to save with Harresh (or try to)

      Ormagon is pretty much the only one for creatures but just play conservatively there are really no creatures that are used for specific magi only.

      As many spells as possible especially Impacts and Crushing Sands as they will benefit Ythra more than Harresh, you will see when I get to her.

      As for relics try not to use up all of the warriors boots, I know harresh is supposed to go all out but do not use up all of the resources in the deck.

      Ythra - She is a powerhouse when it comes to spells. Take the sunburn starting and by now you should a decent hand with some crushing sands. Ythras strategy is to Clear the board with Spells then using either an impact or warriors boots remove all the energy from an opposing magi. Try not to use ormagon. Your probably wondering why I have the underneath cards in here and that is because the impacts are unbelievably good and the ormagon is a splash cards for a lot of decks but I have an underneath/dresh magi third so I don’t have to worry about regional penalties. Anyways back to Ythra. I think she is pretty self explanatory. Clear the board and use one of the cards in this deck to remove all the energy from an opposing magi, its as simple as that! And she does this very well, believe me.

      Cards to save with ythra

      Ormagon, one sikra, some impacts, and at least one climbing staff but that isn't important

      D'jarvish - He's a great finisher for this deck because he is half underneath, that means ill have more energy to spend if I need to use one of the underneath cards. Starting a sikra isn't bad either, it’s a great creature for stalling with or impacting, Illusions are for impacting in this deck. D'jarvish also adds 1 energy to his illusions that attack and that is always good so your creatures can stay big enough to impact for the win. A lot of the time you don’t even need impact just play warriors boots in a bulabantu to kill of a creature and then have every other creature you have attack. My opponent almost never gets past Ythra but if they were skilled enough and you played the way I explained D'jarvish may be able to Cleanup. If you ever got to him with them only on their second magi then you'll want to play him more like Ythra with spells and "toy with our opponent until they run out of resources or spells to back up their creatures. Then Use those big guns (xala,sandstorm orshaa etc.) to Clear the board, just do this until you wear them down and kill them off!
      CARD BREAKDOWN


      Harresh - Draw

      Ythra - Ultimate Beatdown

      D'jarvish - Cleanup

      3 Olum Digger - Great Power, gets targeted a lot and could be used as a decoy to protect another creature of theirs.
      3 Uban - Discard, and recurring to make sure you have some non illusions
      3 Bulabantu- ....These rule!
      3 Darbok- Discard and I need non-illusions creatures
      2 Xala - Stops ormagons and other threats/ its big!
      2 Sikra- Stalling card and good with ormagon also just a cheap creature
      1 ormagon - Clears the board
      1 Sandstorm orshaa - Illusion pretty much there so I can impact that instead of a xala, good for attacking too.

      3 Unmake - Discard
      3 Crushing Sands- Discard and great side effect
      3 Impact - What makes the deck win a lot
      3 Sunburn - Staple for all dresh
      3 Forgotten dreams - Bring back those warriors boots..you also get to draw, I LOVE this card
      1 Heat of day - just in case everything is a little too big (and bograth =P)

      3 Warriors boots - .........
      2 Climbing staff - Save you from opponents impacts , spirit drain etc.
      1 Bottled City - use with Harresh, awesome card
