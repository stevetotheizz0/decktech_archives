This deck will get rocked in the tournament scene. The numbers killer will completely shut it down. This Deck has no Darth Vader and thus will never flip. Vader is a destiny 1 character and I don’t want any destiny 1’s in this deck. Now that all the negative stuff is mentioned it does not need to be posted on the reviews.

This deck is a direct damage manipulative deck and can be fun when played as a pickup game. It starts out turn one deploying the emperor to the throne room and deploying emperors power there as well. Your goal in the early game is just to deploy 3 characters including Palpy. It is best if you keep them deployed to the throne room. This is your power base make your opponent come to you. If he comes with a spy Fry him with force lightning. Once down insert the numbers. and draw for your counter Assaults. Other than your 4 locations there are no destinies under three.

If your opponent doesn’t play your insight serves you well you will have an advantage. After a numbers or two has popped start deploying the Jabba’s Palce location and pull the other sites. Use none shall pass to drain with your weak characters and get out Mi’oom w/ the AC text is she is not out already. Put a Tuskin in the palace w/ a gaffi stick. You can now play imperial artillary. Use other direct damage as needed.

The Tuskins are really to delay Podracing. The longer you strech out the race the better. The other positive thing is that you are not running a racer so you can not be A step backwards’ed If you can hit his high destinies you actually have a slim chance of winning the race. The abyssin ornaments are in the deck to pull the raiders NOT to retrieve. You can not retrieve with this deck unless you track a 0 and that is hard to do.

Why Gaffi sticks? Eventually the opponent will break through your NSP defense. drawing a 6 or better should be pretty easy in this deck (remember only for cards in your deck are below destiny 3 and GS draws 2 destiny.) This will prevent EPP mania from hitting everything that moves if he gets to battle you and causing you to lose massive amounts of characters at a site.

The late game you will most likely be running away from thier hit force which is fine. if you can safely split your group go ahead and sneak in a drain or two. Limited resources and voyer finish the job for you if not a last second numbers can.

Of course all of this is irrelevant if your opponent plays Your Insight Serves you Well. In which case try to hit him with whatever Direct Damage you can and drain at JP as quickly as you can.

Finaly this deck will work only once in a very long while. After you have unleashed the direct damage horde, everyone will tech for numbers.

Say Cheese :P
