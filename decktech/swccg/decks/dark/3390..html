Anyway, here's the strategy ...
Drain for big on Tatooine, while causing direct damage with the objective and Search and Destroy. If they come to play, obliterate them. Expand a drain +1 site (preferably Lars Farm for the bonus weapon destinies) and add PotF's and Sabers for super drains ('drain 5 at the Cantina, 4 at the Lars' Farm, and 3 at the Jundland Wastes ...'). Sense/Alter everything in sight.

FAQ
'TWO 4LOMs? TWO Iggys?'
Damn straight. These guys are the joint, and no one expects you to be playing with two of em. Plus they get forfeit bonus from the objective.

'ONE Mara?'
I get her with AC text first turn (this also establishes Sense/Alter). Then she stays on table. Don't need more than one.

'Why not just play Scum and Villainy?'
Cuz I like Vader! And Tarkin, and Igar, and walkers etc. Rather have them than S&V.

'Danz Borin? Brangus Glee??'
Put Danz on a walker and he adds 3 to power and is forfeit 6 (with Jabba at the AC). That's better than Evax, and he's better destiny and cheaper deploy if Chall is around. Brangus kicks in the Cantina, or on a walker too.

'Why Battle Order when your space is so weak and you don't even have a BG system?'
Battle Order is for ops, plain and simple. If they play ops, I start Battle Order, get my BH ships out to their system (maybe a chump or two as forfeit fodder also), then proceed as normal on Tatooine. It'll be a drain race, and I'll win cuz I won't be paying for drains :-)
I don't use Battle Order every game.

'Why no Security Precautions?'
Frankly, I couldn't find room. Sucks, but I can do without Sec Precs. Just cuz it ain't out doesn't mean I can't probe!

'Why this particular Vader mix?'
My destiny isn't too hot, so I use two Premiere to help my BDs out. Choker isn't gonna get too many people anyway. If I'm feeling gutsy, or have been tracking destinies, I'll bring Choker out. Otherwise, Premiere is the way to go.

'How does it do against certain deck types?'
Well ...
Against HB - I leave my Twilek in my deck, and use no starting interrupt. First turn, Mara goes to the Chamber, next turn she gets her Saber for a drain of 2. I lock down two sites and get Search and Destroy (using the Twilek if I draw it) ASAP. As soon as he flips, I send my ships out from RD (or from hand if I drew any) and probe. I can probe twice per ship (once during my turn, then move and probe during his turn) before he has a chance to kill me (I've got Sense to take care of Barriers). Probing usually doesn't take more than two turns. I've got two Fett in Slaves so when he gets killed the first time I can use him again (another double that no one expects). If I think I can swing it, I'll stack my ships up at Kessel after probing to stop him from draining there (maybe give em some characters for forfeit fodder). But usually I'll outdrain him on Tatooine and cause him problems with S&D and the objective. Got Snipers for UC spies and Alters for Projections.
In general, I don't try to fight HB in space - please keep that in mind. I just send my ships out on suicide 'probe and run' missions, and rely on my ground drains to win for me.

Against MWYHL - I start Bad Feeling to slow him, then get Failure out as soon as I can (it usually comes out right before they finish Test 2, or right after). Obviously my Senses and Alters are limited, but I am usually able to pull off a fair share.

Against Profit - I start Bad Feeling again, and put Chall and Mara at the AC. Take the Engineer into hand with Chall. First turn, deploy him and convert the AC. From there I either: use the AC to get Elephant Man to keep Lando, Artoo, Boussh etc. out; OR move everyone to JP where I can drain for 3 (Mara's Saber) until he rescues Han, then go kill/capture Han. If I can capture Han with IG, the game's over. Sense SWLY and all the other little interrupts they use. 4LOM Ben. Kick the crap outta him.

Against Operatives - See above

Against numbers - Resistance handles it. I chose it over Tortures because I didn't want any 6 destinies screwing with my S/A. Search and Destroy is fun too ;-)
