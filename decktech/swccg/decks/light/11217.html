StrategyWell, it’s just like I said in my description. Drain on the land with the mains, and use Squadron Assignments to get the pilots in their ships to man the clouds. The Klatonnian Revol. are brute power, and can be used for either Noble Sacrifice or Nar Shadda for retrieval. Figrin is a good way to get little but constant retrieval.
