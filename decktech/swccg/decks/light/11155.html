StrategyWell this deck does fairly well and is fairly fast. You can get to test three within your first four or fives turns, which is a must to get it down right. Start with Wise Adivise and for sure The Way of things. The third effect needs to be insurrection for the docking bay support but can change. I found it hard to make a good and workable Jedi Test deck because it is so effect and interrupt intensive, but I think I got enough in there. I used docking bays so that I can use heavy hitters like Ben, Luke and Han to run around and make a mess of the opponent while Leia stays back and helps you out. The air is powerful and effective and can really get the job done with a small amount of support.
