Strategy the number of interrupts should be 20, not 23 and the character number is 14, not 13. Swap It's a Trap for Mantellian Savrip, i'm 1 card short, that card should be SomeOne Who Loves You

This deck has been very effective. It's still in playtesting, but just by the looks of it, YOU KNOW IT'S GOOD.

Why only 2 Massa Lukes and 2 Jedi Lukes? why not 3-4 MASSAs and 0-1 Jedi Lukes?
Well, mainly because, I like how Master Luke deploys for free on Tatooine. But we all know Jedi Luke is 10x, better, we just don't like paying 8 for him. The Tunnel Visions will help you out greatly. Get out Massa Luke for free, drop Jedi Luke + stick as a replacement, and boom, jedi luke on tatooine for free and all for just 1 lost force, sweet, eh?

Why Dodge over Narrow Escape? Why no Rebel Barrier?
Simple. Blast Door Controls is a widely used card now. Plain and simple, I've found a way around it (others have used these cards too). Even with my SAC (hehe) almost every DS plays with Oppressive Enforcement, so it would hit the table again.

Won't BHBM eat you alive thought?
No....but Profit is weak to BHBM. I love BHBM (playing it...not against it) and I beat a Profit by +28 today, but he played it bad. You do not want to rescue Han with Luke. This was one big mistake my friend (opponent) made at our tourny game. Out comes Vader, grabs Luke, and @#$%-slaps Han for a lot of force (with Emperor's Power out). You should prolly use Ben to rescue Han and Gift of the Mentor should get out his stick. I guess a Sorry About the Mess would be good if they moved over to get Luke. You could put Luke at Ben + Han's site. So if Vader is elsewhere and moves over, you could lay the beats. Use your opponent's objective's text to get out Jedi Luke from deck to Tatooine. Use Dodge to avoid *sticky* situations. Just keep Han alive. Once your opponent has luke, he will want to duel. So you just gotta worry about battles. Don't make any stupid battles, like Ben vs. Mara and Blizzard 2. You'll lose if they got Emperor's Power. Stack 1 on Insignificant Rebellion and that just sucks. BHBM is prolly the ONE DS deck that will beat Profit 7 times outta 10.

ROPs gets eaten alive. Endor Ops....what a joke. Rumors is decent, but if you flip early enuff, you should be fine. The deck wins....no doubt about it....just watch out for BHBM, with Luke gone, things can get rough. It shouldn't be hard to clear the AC. If they play antyhing, But BHBM, use TFSWTO to help kill guys with Luke and you can flip with just luke. Yavin 4 Occupation....damage race in which you will win cuz of you're exceptionally faster pace. Savrip helps a ton. Grab Imperial Command if they play it.


Cards I would like to add:

^More space - Like RS1 and DS2 Wedge or something

Give it a try, it's fairly standard, but solid as a rock
