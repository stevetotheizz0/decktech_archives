Basic:
Okay your turn grab the Home One Docking bay and drop a Bothan there. Draw. Keep them under control until you’re set up for the inserts. Ideally you only have Artoo, Luke and a Spy but it doesn’t work like that most of the time. Place spies undercover except where they have scrubs. That’s where you can Surprise Assault. They can grab everything but you can still play them, and you have the force to. If they grab one of the Nar Shaddas then save the other for retreival.

Match-ups:
Against TIEs (I’ve played 2 TIE Decks) Surprise Assault when you can. Set-up a drain with Luke and saber, and one at the Y4:Throne Room with Revolution.

Against Hunt Down: Undercover with Vader, Spies on the executor, TT Visage...Insert Boom they’re dead. If the set-up is correct early on you can track so that when they duel they lose the duel. I only do this if I feel very confidant and they don’t have Tarkin there or available.

Against BHBM: Drop Luke. Wait till they deploy more 1 destiny characters to insert. DON’T BE STUPID and insert when Palpy is one of their three lowest destiny characters. This is a hard match-up but their own damage can kill them.

Against Scum: Grab Projective. If they play Skrillings watch their numbers. You can still win surprise assaults if you’re careful.

Against Anything Else: Play Smart and you shall win.
