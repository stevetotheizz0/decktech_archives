    AT-ST Operations/ Endor is Mine

    Strategy
    This is my version of a flipping Endor Ops deck. It is a drain deck, which tries to hold sites to give you a drain of 5 at the system using Establish Secret Base. I do not use Ominous Rumors in this deck, I only have it in there so I can flip. I made this deck mainly because I wanted a switch up for DS, as I mostly only play Deal and CCT. Any suggestions are appreciated because it seems to be a little card intensive right now, so it could probably use a little help. Also the reason I decided to go with all AT-ST’s over biker scouts is because if I went with biker scouts it would have ended up being even more card intensive. I know that AT-ST’s cost more force, but I think they give me more power overall.




    Basic Strategy:




    First Turn: Activate 5 plus whatever opponent gives you. First off you want to get out some sites. Use LLL (v) to get out a site and use Conduct Your Search, if you choose to start that, to get out the Back Door. Even though I don’t really have any scouts in this deck, I start that when I need quicker activation. Use your objective to get Ominous Rumors and depending on your match up deploy it right away. Probably the only two decks that could cancel it on you second turn would be TRM and RST. Also pull the Executor with the Mob Points combo if they don’t suspend it and then you can either draw up or save some force depending on what’s in your hand.




    Second Turn: Activate at least 7 (usually 9). Use 2 force to get out your last site, and then deploy some AT-ST’s. Also get out Establish Secret Base. Ditch Mob Points combo for Perimeter Patrol.




    Third Turn: Activate at least 9 (usually 11). If you didn’t start CYS, then get out another site, but from here on I am just going to give the strategy with starting CYS. Hopefully you can get in a drain or two on this turn. If you have a copy of Twi’lek in your hand use it to try and get either Alert My Star Destroyer (v) or Imperial Domination (v). Now you have 8 force left. Deploy some more AT-ST’s, and save what force you can to deploy the Executor or another SD to Endor next turn. The first few turns you will probably take some heavy ground damage if you aren’t careful so use your Barrier’s and Ghhhk’s accordingly. If you can try and get someone to the Bunker so that Perimeter Patrol gives you a built in Barrier and some direct damage.




    Fourth Turn: Activate 11. If all is going your way, drain where you can and deploy a SD to Endor and then deploy Establish Secret Base and flip. Now your AT-ST’s have some immunity and you get some uncancellable drains on Endor. Plus your opponent only gets 1 battle destiny per battle.




    Rest of Game: Try and get out all of your SD’s with matching pilots aboard to Endor, so that you can hold the system against pretty much any LS space package. Getting two battle destinies (even if one gets cancelled it won’t matter much), and decent immunity is great. Try and control sites on Endor as best you can so you can keep the drain of 5 at the system. Also drain as much as possible at your sites. Your AT-ST’s give you pretty decent power, so you shouldn’t have much trouble holding at least 2 sites if not 3. Retrieve lost characters with Imperial Domination (v) whenever you can as well. Also try and get Col Dyer to the Bunker to prevent Ominous Rumors from being cancelled.




    Card Choices:


    Battle Deployment - Lets you pull Combat Vehicles and even if you aren’t flipped, stops opponent from drawing more than one battle destiny in battles on Endor while you occupy the system.


    Commander Praji (v) – Lets you pull your AT-ST pilots


    Take Evasive Action (v) – Protection against Speeders


    I would like to add Security Precautions (v) and Closed Door, but I’m not sure what to take out. Any ideas?




    Matchups:


    RST: Deploy to the Landing Site and try and kill their guys with your AT-ST cannons. Also make sure you don’t let those pesky Z-95’s take the system. I also have Vader w/ Saber and The Emperor in here to help protect the bunker.


    QMC: It is going to basically be a drain race. Use Endor Occupation to cancel Cloud City Celebration and stop their retrieval.


    TRM: Haven’t actually played it, but I am fairly sure it will give you trouble. They will be able to deploy more guys, and block your drains. At least they can’t target your vehicles with their EPP’s. This game will be very interactive. So make sure not to leave a single AT-ST alone.


    Pretty much anything else will also end up being a drain race unless they bring the fight to you. Your best bet is to stay on Endor no matter what, because your forces will be spread too thin if you go to their sites. Stick to your own game plan and you should have a decent chance.




    Thats about it. Again, I appreciate any suggestions and I hope you enjoyed this deck.
